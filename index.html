I am a second-year PhD student in the Computer Science Department, University of California, Los Angeles. I become a member of Center for Vision, Cognition, Learning and Autonomy, 2019. My supervisor is Professor Song-Chun Zhu. Before that, I received my Science Master degree in the Department of Computer Science and Technology of Tsinghua University, supervised by Professor Jianyong Wang. I received my B.S. degree from the School of Automation, 2015.

I was a research intern with the NLC Group, MSRA from Nov. 2017 to May 2018, working with Dr. Lei Ji, Nan Duan, and Ming Zhou. Before that, I was a visiting student with Multimedia Laboratory, CUHK from Aug 2016 to Oct 2017, working with Professor Hongsheng Li and Xiaogang Wang.

I am interested in computer vision and natural language understanding, especially in commonsense reasoning for vision, language, knowledge, and cognition.
